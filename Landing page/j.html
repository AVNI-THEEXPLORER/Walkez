Rate Streets

Give ratings to streets based on their walkability, and help fellow pedestrians.

Upload Photos/Videos

Upload images or videos of streets to showcase problems like cracks or blocked pathways.

View a live heatmap of Bengaluru's walkability, powered by community ratings.
